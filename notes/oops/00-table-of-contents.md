## Prerequisites

This guide assumes:
- 10+ years of software development experience
- Strong understanding of at least one OOP language (Java, C#, Python, etc.)
- Experience with enterprise application development
- Familiarity with distributed systems concepts

## How to Use This Guide

1. **Start with fundamentals** if you need a refresher
2. **Jump to specific topics** for targeted learning
3. **Review complex scenarios** to understand practical applications
4. **Refer to real-world examples** for industry context

## Author Notes

This guide is designed for senior developers who:
- Need to architect complex systems
- Want to make informed design decisions
- Are preparing for technical leadership roles
- Need to mentor junior developers on best practices
